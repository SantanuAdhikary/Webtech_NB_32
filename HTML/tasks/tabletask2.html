Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 ° F
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
flour 2 cups
sugar 2 cups
chocolate chips 1 cups

Enjoy your homemade cookies!
